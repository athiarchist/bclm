His Journey

The Story Behind BCLM

From an uncertain fate at auction to roaming free in green meadows. Here is how his life was saved.

1
The Turning Point

Saved From Slaughter

At the auction block, his gentle eyes made it impossible to walk away. Instead of heading to processing, he was purchased on the spot and brought to safety.

2
First Day Home

Stepping Into Freedom

Unloading off the trailer into fresh pasture grass for the first time. Unsure of what open space meant, he slowly felt green grass under his hooves.

3
Pasture Pet Life Today

Living Like Royalty

Now he spends his days sunbathing, enjoying fresh apple treats, getting chin scratches, and inspiring people everywhere that every black steer matters.
